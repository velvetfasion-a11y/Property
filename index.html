Heritage

A Century of Privacy

Strandöen has passed through a single lineage since its establishment as a private domain.

1897

The island is formally established as a private estate within the Djursholm archipelago, one of the earliest recorded private island holdings in the Stockholm region.

1920s

The main residence is completed in the Nordic National Romantic style, designed to disappear into the forest canopy and granite terrain.

1960s

Infrastructure modernised discreetly — private dock, generator systems, and telecommunications — without altering the island's essential character.

Today

Strandöen enters a new chapter. Offered privately to a select audience of qualified buyers who understand the distinction between property and sovereignty.
